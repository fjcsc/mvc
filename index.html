Portuguese Teacher for
English speaking students

I just love teaching and learning new languages. Our goal here is to follow a few easy steps so anyone can start speaking Portuguese.

About Me

I'm a native Brazilian foreign language educator, born and living in Rio de Janeiro . I do have a bachelor degree in Portuguese Language and Literature from the University Santa Ursula (RJ). I'm also a grandmother (spectacular credential) and have been teaching students from different ages and knowledge for more than 40 years.

I like to works with individuals and corporate training programs. In addition to Portuguese, I can speak English and French. Due to my extensive career, I feel more than capable of helping my students overcome cultural differences they might encounter during their learning experience, as well as while doing business in Brazil or simply traveling or living over here.

Teaching Style

I'll be teaching you throght SKYPE so it requeires a method and some "discipline" from both of us.

I utilize a method called “Effective Total Immersion”; this means the student is immersed directly and immediately into the target language from the opening day of class. I start speaking Portuguese slowly and clearly and use easily understandable cognates.

My method concentrates on speaking only in Portuguese, while I use hand movements or signals as well as visual images to convey the language in a proper manner. Grammar, reading, and writing are also part of the method, without being tedious or repetitive. I use textbooks especially formulated for teaching Brazilian Portuguese for foreigners. Students learn the language without analyzing or trying to translate into their native language. I believe the key to learning a new language relies mostly on verbal communication.

I strongly encourage my students to work on assignments at home, which we correct together during the following class. It is also important to devote time to reading, writing, and personal research. This outside the classroom work is a crucial part of any language learning process.

Whether you are a beginner or an advanced speaker, I am highly trained to help build your confidence and keep you motivated to achieve your goals!
